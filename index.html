Polio needs our attention

Poliomyelitis (polio) is a highly infectious viral disease , that largely affects children under 5 years of age. The virus is transmitted by person-to-person, spread mainly through contaminated water or food and multiplies in the intestine, from where it can invade the nervous system and cause paralysis.
